Biomedical Waste Categories

Yellow Bin: Infectious, anatomical waste, soiled dressings, masks, blood bags, placenta, etc.
Red Bin: Contaminated plastics like tubing, catheters, IV sets, vacutainers, urine bags (if plastic), etc.
White (Translucent) Bin: Sharps like needles, scalpels, scissors, blades.
Blue Bin: Glassware, vials, ampoules, metallic implants, expired medicines (non-cytotoxic as per policy).
Green Bin: Non-infectious general waste like paper, food leftovers (as per segregation policy).
