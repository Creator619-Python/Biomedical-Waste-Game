Biomedical Waste Categories

Yellow Bin: Human anatomical waste, soiled dressings, blood bags, placenta, masks, etc.
Red Bin: Contaminated plastics like IV sets, catheters, urine bags, tubing, vacutainers.
White (Translucent) Bin: Sharps such as needles, scalpels, blades, scissors.
Blue Bin: Glassware, vials, ampoules, metallic implants, some expired medicines (as per policy).
Green Bin: General non-infectious waste like paper/food (as per hospital segregation policy).
